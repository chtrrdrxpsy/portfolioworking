About Me

Hi! I'm Eric Hatter, and welcome to my personal portfolio website. I’ve been involved in the Ican program for over five years, and through it, I’ve discovered my passion for mechanics and working with cars.

Throughout my time in Ican, I have worked on a variety of hands-on projects, which have deepened my love for mechanics. I’m particularly passionate about working on cars, specifically fixing and restoring High end cars although I understand I won't be able to start at that level. This passion, combined with the skills I’ve gained in Ican, has fueled my desire to work in a garage and pursue a career as a mechanic in the future.

Although the journey has had its challenges, the Ican program has been instrumental in shaping my career path. Not only has it taught me technical skills, but it has also helped me form lasting relationships and provided me with job opportunities that I truly value. I’m excited to continue learning and growing in this field, and I hope to stay connected with the people I’ve met along the way.
